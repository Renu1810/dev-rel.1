// FAQ

Common questions.

Do I need DevRel if I'm pre-launch?+
Yes — pre-launch is the best time to start. Building developer relationships before you need them means a warm audience on launch day. DevRel is far easier to build proactively than retroactively.
How does the DevRel agent actually work?+
We train a custom Claude-powered agent on your docs, GitHub, product changelog, and community conversations. It answers developer questions, qualifies leads, and handles onboarding — deployed as a chat widget on your site or as a bot in your Discord.
What's the difference between DevRel and developer marketing?+
Developer marketing is campaigns and top-of-funnel. DevRel is the ongoing relationship — trust, education, feedback loops, community. The best DevRel feeds your marketing, but it's built on genuine developer value, not conversion goals.
How quickly can the agent go live?+
Typically 5–10 business days from kickoff to live deployment — faster if your docs are well-structured. We handle all the training, tuning, and deployment setup.
Do you work with non-AI companies?+
NullSpace specializes in AI and data companies, but works with any tech company where developers are a core audience — data infra, developer tools, API-first products. If developers use your product, DevRel matters.
What does a first call look like?+
It's a free 30-minute call on Topmate. We'll talk about your product, your current developer touchpoints, and what's working or not. You'll leave with at least one concrete action regardless of whether we work together.
